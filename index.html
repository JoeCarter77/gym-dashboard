How To Use This Dashboard

Tracks your full revenue pipeline from lead to member. See exactly where money is leaking and what fixing it is worth.

1

Create your account

Register with your email and password. Your data is private to your account — nobody else can see it.

2

Set up your membership tiers

Go to Enter Data → Membership Setup. Add each tier with its monthly price and a label. Set your Average LTV — the total lifetime value of a single member.

3

Enter your monthly numbers

Select a month and fill in: Ad Spend, Extra Expenses, Total Leads, Appointments Booked, Appointments Showed, and Memberships Sold per tier.

4

Read your dashboard

See ROAS, conversion rates at every funnel stage, cost per lead, cost per conversion, and your Chaos Tax — the LTV your marketing already paid for but your follow-up lost.

5

Data saves automatically

Everything saves to the cloud as you type. Log out and back in from any device and your numbers are there. Export CSV anytime for a full snapshot.

Glossary
Front End Revenue
Actual membership revenue from new sign-ups this month.
Projected Monthly Run Rate
New members x LTV. Total lifetime value generated by this month's sign-ups — what they're really worth over their full time with you.
Front End ROAS
Front end revenue ÷ ad spend. Shows if your ads pay for themselves immediately.
Back End ROAS
Projected run rate ÷ ad spend. The true long-term return on your ad investment.
Booking Rate
% of leads who book a trial. Low = follow-up problem.
Show Up Rate
% of bookings who show up. Low = confirmation/reminder problem.
Close Rate
% of shows who bought. Low = sales process problem.
Chaos Tax
LTV lost at each funnel stage. Money your ads already paid for that your follow-up dropped.

Quick Start

Step 1: Enter Data → set up your membership tiers and LTV.

Step 2: Select a month, fill in your leads, spend, and memberships sold.

Step 3: Check the Dashboard for ROAS, conversion rates, and Chaos Tax.

Step 4: Everything saves automatically to the cloud as you type.
